Burgers and Recipes

Check out some of our favorite burger recipes:

  • Classic Cheeseburger

    Ingredients:

    • Ground beef
    • Cheese
    • Buns
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Ketchup
    • Mustard
    • Salt and pepper

    Directions:

    1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
    2. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Grill burgers for about 4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
    4. Add cheese to burgers during last minute of grilling.
    5. Toast buns on grill.
    6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, ketchup, and mustard.

  • Bacon Cheeseburger

    Ingredients:

    • Ground beef
    • Bacon
    • Cheese
    • Buns
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Mayonnaise
    • Salt and pepper

    Directions:

    1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy, then remove from pan and set aside.
    2. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
    3. Cook burgers in same skillet as bacon over medium-high heat for about 4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
    4. Add cheese to burgers during last minute of cooking.
    5. Toast buns in oven or on grill.
    6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, mayonnaise, and bacon.
