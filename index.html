Classic Burger

Our classic burger is made with a juicy beef patty, American cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, all served on a sesame seed bun. Add bacon or extra cheese for an additional charge.

Veggie Burger

Our veggie burger is made with a house-made veggie patty, cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, all served on a brioche bun. Add avocado or grilled mushrooms for an additional charge.

Bacon Burger

Our bacon burger is made with a juicy beef patty, crispy bacon, cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, all served on a brioche bun. Add a fried egg or jalapenos for an additional charge.
